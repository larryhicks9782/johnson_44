## Full Description (4000 characters max)

### What is GCommunity?
GCommunity is a mobile app designed to help you discover meaningful local gatherings and build connections in your community. Whether you're interested in arts, wellness, learning, nature, food, or social events, GCommunity makes it easy to find gatherings that match your interests.

### Key Features

🎯 **Discover Gatherings**
- Browse local community events and gatherings
- Filter by category: Arts & Culture, Wellness, Social, Learning, Nature, Food
- See gathering details: date, time, location, capacity, host info
- View attendee lists and join communities

🗺️ **Interactive Map**
- Visualize gatherings on an interactive map
- See locations, distances, and directions
- Discover nearby events at a glance

📱 **QR Code Check-In**
- Hosts generate QR codes for gathering check-ins
- Guests scan QR codes to confirm attendance
- Integrated analytics for host insights

💡 **AI-Powered Ideas**
- Get AI-generated gathering suggestions based on your interests
- Discover creative icebreaker questions for hosts
- Personalized recommendations

📊 **Host Analytics**
- Track QR code scans and attendance
- Monitor scan history with detailed timeline
- Analyze gathering performance

📝 **Event Management**
- Create new gatherings with full details
- Set capacity limits and scheduling
- Send reminders to attendees
- Export gatherings as PDF

🎨 **User Profiles**
- Create your community profile
- Share your interests and bio
- Track joined gatherings
- Build your local network

### How It Works

1. **Sign in** with your Google account
2. **Browse** gatherings on the map or in your feed
3. **RSVP** to gatherings you want to attend (attending/maybe)
4. **Receive** reminders before events
5. **Scan** QR codes at gatherings to check in
6. **Rate** gatherings and leave comments
7. **Host** your own gatherings and invite friends
