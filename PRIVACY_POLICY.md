# Privacy Policy for GCommunity

**Last Updated:** June 26, 2026

## Overview
GCommunity ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise process information in connection with our mobile application and related services.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Information You Provide Directly
- **Account Information:** Name, email address, profile picture, bio, interests
- **Gathering Information:** Titles, descriptions, locations, dates, times
- **Communications:** Messages, comments, RSVP information
- **Location Data:** Coordinates for gathering locations (with your permission)

### B. Information Automatically Collected
- **Device Information:** Device type, OS version, app version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** Features used, pages visited, time spent, tap events
- **Crash Reports:** Error logs and performance metrics (via Firebase Crashlytics)
- **Analytics:** User behavior patterns, event tracking (via Firebase Analytics)
- **Camera/QR Data:** QR code scan results (processed locally, not stored)

### C. Third-Party Information
- **Google Sign-In:** Email, display name, profile picture
- **Firebase:** Authentication and cloud storage data

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and improve the GCommunity service
- Authenticate users and manage accounts
- Display relevant gatherings and community information
- Send notifications and reminders about gatherings
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Detect and prevent fraud or security issues
- Comply with legal obligations
- Analyze usage patterns and trends

## 3. Data Sharing

We **do not sell** your personal information. We share data only in these cases:

### A. Service Providers
- **Firebase (Google):** Authentication, database, analytics
- **Gemini AI (Google):** Generating gathering suggestions (prompts don't store personal data)

### B. Legal Requirements
- Court orders, warrants, or government requests
- Protecting safety, security, or legal rights

### C. Public Information
- Gathering hosts are visible to other users
- Attendee lists are visible to gathering hosts
- Public profile information is visible to other users searching for gatherings

## 4. Data Security

We implement:
- HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
- Firebase security rules restricting database access
- Environment variable protection for API keys
- Regular security audits
- No sensitive data stored locally unencrypted

**Note:** No security system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 5. Your Rights & Choices

You can:
- **Access:** Request all data we hold about you
- **Delete:** Request deletion of your account and data
- **Opt-out:** Disable analytics/crash reporting in app settings
- **Revoke:** Remove app permissions (Camera, Location) in device settings

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@gcommunity.app

## 6. Data Retention

- **Active Accounts:** Data retained while account is active
- **Inactive Accounts:** Deleted after 12 months of inactivity
- **Analytics:** Aggregated data retained for 25 months
- **Crash Logs:** Retained for 90 days

## 7. Children's Privacy

GCommunity is not intended for children under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we learn we've collected such data, we'll delete it promptly.

## 8. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

### For EU Users (GDPR)
- Your data is your property. We process it only with consent or legal basis
- Right to access, correct, delete, or port your data
- Right to lodge complaints with your data protection authority

### For California Users (CCPA)
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of data sales (we don't sell data)
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights
